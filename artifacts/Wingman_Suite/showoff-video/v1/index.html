Positioning

Lead with outcomes, then architecture.

The video should not start as a product tour. It should start as a problem solved: AI work should not die in a chat window.

  • Primary line: AI that actually runs work.
  • Category: AI operations hub for real businesses.
  • Proof: tasks, agents, apps, memory, approvals, results.

Visual Rules

Make it feel like systems, not magic.

The visual rhythm should be editorial, practical, and direct, with product surfaces and workflow diagrams doing the heavy lifting.

  • Use real UI captures in the final cut where possible.
  • Keep labels short enough to read in two seconds.
  • Show motion through state changes, not decorative effects.
