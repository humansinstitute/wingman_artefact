Putting it together

Wingman is for orchestrating work across agents and people.

The point is not just smarter chat. It is a shared operating layer where agents can execute, people can review, and teams can coordinate real work across projects.

Autopilot Flight Deck Tower Skills Teams

Team pattern

Many people, many workers, one shared state.

BriefA person creates a task, chat request, flow step, or approval.
AssignAutopilot routes the work to the right agent, repo, app, or pipeline.
ExecuteThe worker edits code, researches, builds artifacts, updates docs, or calls APIs.
ReviewFlight Deck shows status, diffs, comments, decisions, files, and artifacts.
RememberTower keeps records and graph memory for the next worker or person.

What changes

AI becomes operational infrastructure.

Work survives the chat

Tasks, docs, files, state, and artifacts remain visible after the session ends.

Agents specialize

Different workers can handle code, research, review, app operation, content, or customer workflows.

People stay in control

Approvals, task boards, comments, and review surfaces keep consequential decisions human-readable.

Apps join the workflow

WApps can expose custom business tools and data without bloating the main workspace UI.

Identity is explicit

People, apps, and agents can sign actions with their own Nostr identities and access rules.

Knowledge compounds

Skills and graph memory let useful process and context improve over time.
