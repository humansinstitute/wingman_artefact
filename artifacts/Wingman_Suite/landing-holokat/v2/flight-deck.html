02 - Human workspace

Flight Deck is where people steer the work.

It gives the team a shared browser surface for chat, task boards, docs, comments, files, flows, approvals, artifacts, scopes, and app launchers.

Autopilot Flight Deck Tower Skills Teams
What it does

Shows the current work: conversations, task state, docs, decisions, approvals, files, comments, and outputs from agents or people.

Why it exists

Teams need more than a transcript. They need shared state, review surfaces, project context, and a place to decide what happens next.

How it works

Flight Deck talks to Tower's typed APIs and keeps a local browser view fast enough for day-to-day coordination.

Human loop

Flight Deck turns agent output into team workflow.

Chat

Discuss work with people and agents in channels and threads.

Tasks

Track ownership, state, comments, blockers, validation, and review.

Docs

Keep briefs, decisions, notes, handoffs, and design material close to the work.

Approvals

Gate sensitive or consequential steps before downstream work continues.

Artifacts

Review rendered pages, prototypes, screenshots, and generated material with comments.

WApps

Launch custom business apps built for a team, customer, project, or workflow.
